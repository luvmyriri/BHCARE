BHCare Patient Portal User Manual

Welcome to the BHCare Patient Portal. This guide explains how to use the system for the Barangay 174 Health Center.

1. Registration and Login

Registration
- Go to the signup page and click Register.
- You can upload an ID to fill details automatically or type them manually.
- Use a valid email and mobile number.
- Create a password and complete registration.
- You will receive an email with your Patient ID (example: PTNT2026001).

Login
- Use your registered email and password to sign in.

2. Dashboard Overview

Once logged in, the home screen shows:
- Next Appointment: Details of your upcoming visit.
- Recent Records: A summary of your latest medical visit.
- Sidebar: Access all sections from the left menu.

3. Booking Appointments

- Click Appointments in the menu.
- Click Book New Appointment.
- Select the service you need (Consultation, Dental, etc.).
- Choose an available date and time.
- Enter the reason for your visit and submit.

4. Medical Records

- Click Medical Records in the menu.
- View a history of your past visits.
- Click a record to see the diagnosis and prescriptions from the doctor.

5. Document Requests

- Click Document Requests in the menu.
- Click New Request.
- Select the document type (Medical Certificate, Clearance, etc.).
- Enter the purpose and submit.
- The status will update once the staff prepares the document.

6. Settings and Security

Reset Password
- If forgotten, click Forgot Password on the login screen.
- Enter your email to receive a reset link.

Update Profile
- Click Settings in the menu.
- Update your contact info or address and save.

Help
For emergencies, proceed directly to the Health Center. For system help, contact the front desk.
